Course Option

We offer courses in either individual classes or semi-private groups to those who wish to upgrade or maintain their BBB or CCC.

We are here to help you with:

  • Preparation/ Maintaining for level B
  • Preparation/ Maintaining for level CBC/CCC
  • Language Improvement of a particular skill

We offer you course options below:

COURSES : Full-time week days 140 hours per month

  • DAYS: Monday to Friday
  • HOURS: 8:30am to 12:00pm and 1:00pm to 4:30pm
  • MONTHLY COST: $2,590/month
  • LEVELS: Absolute beginners (24 weeks) and Advanced beginners (16 weeks)

COURSES: Part-time week days 36 hours per month

  • DAYS: Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday
  • HOURS: 9:00am-12:00pm OR 1:00pm-4:00pm OR 5:30pm-8:30pm
  • MONTHLY COST: $666/month
  • LEVELS: All levels from beginner to advanced

COURSES: Workshop 7 hours

  • DAYS: week days and weekends
  • HOURS: 8:30am to 12:00pm and 1:00pm to 4:30pm
  • $129 per participant
  • Reading Comprehension and Written Expression Oral Proficiency B and C

Please contact us to find about more options!

In order to allow us to prepare a quote, kindly provide us with your budget and/or the following information: Target Level, Start date, End date, Schedule, Location of training to adm@ecoledeslanguesofficielles.ca

E-Learning

French language training when you want and where you want by videoconference, over the phone, webcam.

Thanks to our team of language coaches you will reach your objectives in terms of second language learning according to your own schedule. Official Languages School has always favored a flexible learning environment according to the schedule of the learner: learn when and where you want in order to optimize the results. It is up to you to decide time and frequency from Monday to Saturday between 9am and 19h.

Once we evaluate your second language level and analyze your needs, we draw up a personalized training program. During language training per se, you will be given a weekly schedule at beginning of each week and a complete summary of the points covered after each session.

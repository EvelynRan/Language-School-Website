For Goverment Employees

We are offering courses to many goverment institutions

Language Training

French language training is delivered by Official Languages School to Federal Government employees preparing for their Second Language Evaluation tests – whether for oral proficiency, written expression, reading comprehension, or all three.

Official Languages School creates a tailored training plan that includes the government PFL2 (French as a Second Language training program developed by the Canada School of Public Service) to help you get your BBB, CBC or CCC levels.

With our high-quality, communicative approach to learning French, we are confident that you will save yourself and your department time, effort, and money. Our highly experienced teachers will accurately estimate the time required before you can reach your desired level. Official Languages School is a Registered Supplier to the government of Canada.

Preparation for the French as a Second Language Evaluation: Group Class and Workshop (full-time or part-time)

Official Languages School group class is the perfect solution for anyone who needs or wants to take the tests of the Second Language Evaluation of the Public Service Commission of Canada to reach level B or C.

Official Languages School workshop is designed to perfect learners’ capability before taking the Public Service Commission tests. It includes a presentation on how the test is customarily administered along with an overview of some useful communication strategies and practical exercises on second-language comprehension, expression and interaction.

This unique training program that has a success rate of around 99%, due to Official Languages School’s unique blended methodology strategy, covers all aspects of linguistic competency, including listening, speaking reading and writing.

Official Languages School is a Registered Supplier to the government of Canada.

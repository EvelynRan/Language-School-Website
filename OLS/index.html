Government Employees

Federal Government Language Training

French language training is delivered by Official Languages School to Federal Government employees preparing for their Second Language Evaluation tests – whether for oral proficiency, written expression, reading comprehension, or all three.

Official Languages School is a Registered Supplier to the government of Canada. It creates a tailored training plan that includes the government PFL2 (French as a Second Language training program developed by the Canada School of Public Service) to help you get your BBB, CBC or CCC levels.

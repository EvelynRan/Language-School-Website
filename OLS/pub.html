For the General Public

We offer courses in either individual classes or semi-private groups to those who wish to upgrade or maintain their BBB or CCC.

French Language Course For Professional Or Cultural Purposes

Discover another culture, meet new people, increase your salary by becoming fluent in French, or simply broaden your language abilities.

Whether your interests are professional or cultural, regardless of your goal, to enhance your international business acumen, become fluent in French for fun, or to travel abroad, Official Languages School provides second language courses of the highest quality. The objectives and course content are defined according to your level and needs: general and business French as well as exam preparation.
